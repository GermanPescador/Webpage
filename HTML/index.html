History

I was camping one day in Teide's Forest Corona, when I thought it would be nice to do some photos of the landscape with my mobile phone and share them with the internet. Since then more than 3 years and hundreds of photos have passed, perfectioning little by little, but there's still a lot to reach mastery. Nowadays I only use my phone when I don't have my camera at hand, a Nikon 3200D with which I capture all sorts of images.
